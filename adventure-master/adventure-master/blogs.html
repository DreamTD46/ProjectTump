Golden Mermaid

Mermaid from legend! Especially Palad Chan often hears this. "Khun Wichitmatra" (Sangakanchanakphan)", one of the important thinkers, researchers, and writers of the Rattanakosin period. Tells a legend about a mermaid that "On a fine day the mermaid will sit beg for hair with a golden comb Sometimes, by chance, a man was able to inspect some of it. The mermaid, in anger, fled into the sea, forgetting her golden comb. The leader saw himself and kept the golden comb and always followed her to the beach. But the mermaid never appeared again.”
